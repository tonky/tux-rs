# 8.3 — Whole Repo Review

**Phase:** 8 — Polish & Packaging
**Depends on:** Phases 1–7 complete
**Unlocks:** 8.5 (Missing Pieces)

## Goal

Systematic review and triage of all postponed items from Phase 6–7 reviews. Each item gets an act/postpone/ignore decision with reasoning. Items marked "act" are implemented in this phase or deferred to 8.5/8.9.

## Approach

1. Launch 2 sub-agents: one for code quality, one for architecture/patterns
2. Each produces findings with severity and recommendations
3. Merge findings, decide on each, document decisions
4. Implement quick fixes (< 30 min each) inline
5. Defer larger items to 8.5 or 8.9
